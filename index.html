Advertise Your Car

If you’d like more eyes on your car, you can submit your details for us to review and potentially advertise on our platform.

Standard listing – £15 for 30 days
Your car will appear on our “cars for sale” area (coming online as we build inventory).
Renewals – £10 per additional 30 days
Keep your advert running if the car hasn’t sold yet.

We’ll ask for clear photos (all four corners, interior, dashboard, wheels) and key details such as mileage, MOT, service history and any known faults.

To get started, contact us by WhatsApp or email and mention that you’d like to advertise your car. We’ll guide you through the next steps.

As this grows, we’ll introduce a simple self-serve upload form, auto-renew reminders and optional “featured listing” upgrades.

Good to know

Do you inspect or buy the car for me?
No – we source suitable listings based on your brief. You handle viewings, checks and purchase directly with the sellers.

Do you guarantee the cars?
No – we don’t inspect or warranty vehicles. We recommend carrying out your own checks and, where appropriate, using a professional inspection.

What areas do you cover?
Online listings across the UK. You can tell us how far you’re prepared to travel.

Can you highlight potential red flags?
We can flag details we notice in the advert or history that may be worth a closer look, but this does not replace your own checks or a professional inspection.

Terms & disclaimer

FindMeACar-UK is a vehicle search and link-finding service. We search online listings based on the information you provide and share vehicles that appear to match your brief.

We do not own, inspect, test or sell any vehicles. All viewings, checks, negotiations, payments and paperwork are your responsibility.

You should always perform your own due diligence before buying any vehicle, including (where appropriate) a professional inspection and history check.
