Good to know

FAQ
Do you inspect or buy the car for me?
No – we source suitable listings based on your brief. You handle viewings, checks and purchase directly with the seller.
Do you guarantee the cars?
No – we don’t inspect or warranty vehicles. We recommend carrying out your own checks and, where appropriate, using a professional inspection.
What areas do you cover?
Online listings across the UK. You can tell us how far you’re prepared to travel.
Can you highlight potential red flags?
We can point out details that stand out in the advert or history that may be worth a closer look, but this does not replace your own checks or a professional inspection.

Terms & disclaimer

Important

FindMeACar-UK is a vehicle search and link-finding service. We search online listings based on the information you provide and share vehicles that appear to match your brief.

We do not own, inspect, test or sell any vehicles. All viewings, checks, negotiations, payments and paperwork are your responsibility.

We do not guarantee condition, history, mileage, price, specification or availability of any vehicle. You should carry out your own checks and, where appropriate, obtain a professional inspection before any purchase.

By using this service, you confirm that you understand and accept these terms.

We aim to donate 1% of each paid search or listing fee to children’s charities annually. This is done quietly in the background – our service is designed to stand on its own, and any donations are simply our way of giving back as we grow.
