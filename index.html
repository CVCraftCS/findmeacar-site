GOOD TO KNOW
Do you inspect or buy the car for me? No – we source suitable listings based on your brief. You handle viewings, checks and purchase directly with the sellers.
Do you guarantee the cars? No – we don’t inspect or warranty vehicles. We recommend carrying out your own checks and, where appropriate, using a professional inspection.
What areas do you cover? Online listings across the UK. You can tell us how far you’re prepared to travel.
Can you highlight potential red flags? We can flag details we notice in the advert or history that may be worth a closer look, but this does not replace your own checks or a professional inspection.

TERMS & DISCLAIMER
